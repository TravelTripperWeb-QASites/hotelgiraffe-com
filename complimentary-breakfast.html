Complimentary Continental Breakfast

Complimentary breakfast is served every morning in the Grand Lobby. Monday - Friday 7 am - 10:30 am; Saturday & Sunday 7 am - 11 am.

Offerings include oatmeal with berries, hard boiled eggs, yogurts, fresh fruit salad and whole fruits, assorted croissants, mini-muffins, scones, Danishes and pastries, bagels with cream cheese, peanut butter, preserves and butter, fresh orange and cranberry juice, assorted cereals, coffee and assorted teas. Our guests are also welcome to have a guest, friend or family member join them for breakfast, with our compliments.

We also offer breakfast delivery if you wish to enjoy your morning in the comfort of your own room. A $5 service fee applies.

If you would like soy milk or gluten-free options, please specify these requests when you make your reservation and we would be happy to provide whatever you need. Please note that these items are not available unless requested.

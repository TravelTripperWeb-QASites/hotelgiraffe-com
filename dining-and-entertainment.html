Dining & Entertainment

Bread & Tulips Restaurant

Bread & Tulips is a cozy neighborhood spot serving rustic Italian cooking in a casual setting located in Hotel Giraffe.

The restaurant combines the flavors and culinary sensibilities of Italian cuisine, with local and seasonal ingredients. The menu showcases rustic Italian favorites such as Crostini , Meatballs, handcrafted Pastas as well as a selection of signature pizza pies, made in Bread & Tulips' state-of-the-art pizza oven, imported from Modena, Italy.

Guests enjoy a 20% discount when dining in the restaurant!

The Rooftop Garden

During the warmer months of the year, The Rooftop Garden at the Hotel Giraffe is the perfect spot to enjoy al fresco drinks under the summer sun or the NYC nighttime sky, in a relaxed and informal setting. The Rooftop Garden is opening depending on the weather, and private bookings, please call ahead to make sure it is open. 212-532-9100.

  • The Rooftop Garden is available for hotel guests only during the daytime, and opens to the public as a rooftop bar Monday-Saturday evenings during the warmer months of the year. The Garden can be rented out for private events during any time of the day, so please always call to ensure that the Garden is open for your enjoyment! Your can also follow us on social media!

The Grand Lobby

Complimentary breakfast is served every morning in the Grand Lobby

Monday - Friday 7 am - 10:30 am; Saturday & Sunday 7 am - 11 am

Assorted croissants, mini-muffins, scones, Danishes and pastries, bagels with cream cheese, peanut butter, preserves and butter, fresh whole fruit and fruit salad, fresh orange and cranberry juice, assorted cereals, oatmeal and yogurts, hard-boiled eggs, coffee and assorted teas. If you would like soymilk or gluten-free bread, please specify these requests when you make your reservation and we would be happy to provide whatever you need. Our guests are also welcome to have a guest, friend or family member join them for breakfast, with our compliments.

We also offer breakfast delivery if you wish to enjoy your morning in the comfort of your own room. A $5 service fee applies.

Guests may enjoy complimentary wine & hors d'oeuvres receptions every evening from 5pm - 8pm. Complimentary afternoon tea, coffee, cookies and whole fruits are available to our guests in the Grand Lobby throughout day

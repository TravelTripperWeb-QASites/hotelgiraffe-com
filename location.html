The Hotel Giraffe Experience

Located on the corner of Park Avenue South and 26th Street

Welcome to an urban oasis located in the heart of New York City. Hotel Giraffe embodies the gentle power, grace and beauty found in one of nature’s most beloved animals. Inspired by the architecture and design of the Art Modern period that has distinguished this vibrant NoMad neighborhood since the 1920’s and 30’s, Hotel Giraffe embodies the graceful elegance, strength, and beauty as well as the legendary kindness found in one of nature’s most beloved animals. This luxury hotel offers timeless style, spacious guestrooms and suites, and most importantly, gracious service that makes it an ideal New York City getaway for leisure and business travelers alike. Experience a world of sophisticated style, unsurpassed comfort and gracious service...compliments of Hotel Giraffe.

One of the many characteristics that set Hotel Giraffe apart from other hotels is the added amenities, which are offered daily.

These offerings include:

  • A continental breakfast including hot oatmeal, boiled eggs, pastries, bagels, muffins, fresh whole fruit and fruit salad with berries, assorted cereals, yogurt, juices, freshly brewed coffee, tea, espresso and cappuccino served daily in the Grand Lobby with a selection of Newspapers
  • Selection of refreshments 24 hours a day
  • Wine & Cheese Reception every evening from 5-8pm with Live Piano Music on Weeknights
  • High speed DSL/Wi-Fi Internet access
  • Passes to New York Sports Club

Evening turndown service, bottled waters and daily newspapers are also included in the room rate.

Meetings & Events

Our retro-glamorous 1,300-square foot Penthouse 1202 provides the ultimate setting for inspiring meetings and unique social events. Spacious and airy, the salon of the Penthouse features a 26ft. vaulted barrel ceiling, panoramic views of Manhattan, a working stone fireplace, private terrace and exquisite landscaped roof-top garden. Penthouse 1202, along with the Park Avenue Terrace and the Roof Garden, can each be rented separately for smaller events, or together for larger events.

Hotel Services

The Grand Lobby at Hotel Giraffe is open to guests of the hotel 24 hours a day. Its plush seating and soothing tones offer an immediate respite upon entering.

Complimentary Wi-Fi is available throughout the entire hotel. We are a non-smoking property. Pets are not allowed. Our complimentary amenities include daily continental breakfast, evening wine and cheese reception every evening, 24 hour reception, all day snacks and refreshments, and passes to New York Sports Club.

Let us know how to make you happy!

Forgot something? Need something extra in your room to be more comfortable? Just let us know! We keep many items on hand for the comfort of our guests. Including:

  • Coffee/Tea Makers
  • Toothbrush
  • Toothpaste
  • Mouthwash
  • Razor
  • Shaving Cream
  • Baby Pack & Play
  • Deodorant
  • Comb
  • and more!

Dry Cleaning & Laundry Service

The hotel offers same day dry cleaning & laundry service for articles handed in before 9:00am Monday to Saturday.

Concierge Service

Our staff delights in nothing more than making your trip to New York everything you hoped for. We customize all our suggestions based on your unique wishes and desires, so give us a bit of insight as to what you are looking for and we will be so pleased to let you know how that experience can be arranged. Tours, theater tickets, transportation, spa appointments including in room massage, restaurant reservations, baby sitting services and so much more can be organized by contacting the concierges at Hotel Giraffe.

Housekeeping

Our staff will service your room between 9:00 am and 4:00 pm. Please contact Front Desk with any special needs.

In-Room Dining

Spectacular In-room dining is provided by Bread & Tulips at Hotel Giraffe. Lunch is available Monday-Friday 12:00pm-3:00pm, and Dinner is available Monday-Wednesday 5:00pm-10:00pm, and Thursday-Saturday 5:00pm-11:00pm. You may also contact the Concierge, by dialing 0, if there are any particular in-room dining requests.

Complimentary Newspapers

Complimentary New York Times, Wall Street Journal, and US Today are available for reading in the Lobby.

Languages

Languages Spoken at Hotel Giraffe: Slovak, Czech, Romanian, French, Spanish, Hungarian, Hindi, Urdu, and of course, English.

Check-in & Check-out

Check in time is 3:00pm. Should you arrive earlier and your room is not as yet ready, we will be happy to store your baggage for you. Check out time is 1:00pm. Please call our reception if your departure is delayed. We will make every effort to accommodate you. We are also happy to store your bags after check out as well. You must be age 21 years or older to check in.

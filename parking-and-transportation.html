Parking & Transportation

Three major airports serve NYC.:

From JFK International Airport:

Go onto Van Wyck Expressway/I-678 North. Take to Grand Central Parkway. From GCP take I-495 (LIE)West exit, merge onto LIE/I-495 West. Follow 495 to Queens Midtown Tunnel. After going through the tunnel, take the 35th street exit on the left towards 34th street/downtown. Turn right on 35th Street to Park Avenue, turn left on Park Avenue, take to 26th, and turn left on 26th.

From Laguardia Airport:

Take the ramp towards Grand Central Parkway West. Keep left at the fork in the ramp to merge onto Grand Central Parkway West. Take the Bklyn/Queens Expressway Exit/exit number 4, towards the Verrazano Narrows Bridge/I-278 West. Take the BQE to I-495 West exit towards Queens Midtown Tunnel. After the tunnel, take the 35th Street exit on the left towards 34th Street downtown. Make a right on 35th St and then turn left on Park Avenue. Turn Left on 26th Street.

From Newark Airport:

Take the I-78 ramp; keep right at the fork in the ramp. Merge onto I-78 East. Take the I-95 North/NJ Turnpike-portions toll-Take exit number 16E, towards Lincoln Tunnel. Merge onto I-495E. I-495 becomes I-495E/Lincoln Tunnel. After going through the tunnel, take the exit on the right towards 36th St. Take 36th Street to Park Avenue. Turn right on Park Avenue. Turn Left on 26th Street.

Transportation

  • From JFK Airport - taxi approximately USD 52.00, plus tolls, plus gratuity.
  • From LGA Airport - taxi approximately USD 35.00, plus tolls, plus gratuity.
  • From Newark Airport - taxi approximately USD 60.00, plus $17.50 surcharge, plus tolls and gratuity.

Parking:

Valet Parking is available for your convenience. The rate is $45 per 24-hour period for any vehicle type. Special transportation arrangements may be made through our concierge prior to your arrival. They may be reached at 212-685-7700.

Additional Information

*Prices subject to change without notice.

*Area transportation can be arranged by the concierge.
